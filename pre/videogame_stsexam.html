Slay the Spire exam

Note: The original version of the exam has been modified with several errata; please see the updated version below.

This is a written exam about the video game Slay the Spire. Why does such a thing exist, you might ask? The main reason is that I thought it would be really funny. Also, I think it's a cool way to share some tactics puzzles and some fun trivia about the game. But mostly it's for the memes.

The exam was released on 2025-08-28. The solutions will be released no earlier than 1 week later (2025-09-04), so that people have time to try it. (I may delay longer, if people are still having fun or if anyone requests it or if I'm busy playing Silksong.) Update: There has been at least one request for an extension, so the actual solutions release date will be later. Further update: Solutions are now released!

If you have a written submission and would like me to check your answers, feel free to send it to me. If you don't want to write things up but just want live commentary as you look at it and answer some of the questions, I would also enjoy hanging out in your STS Discord stream or Twitch chat or whatever, just let me know.
