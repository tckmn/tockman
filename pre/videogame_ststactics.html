This is a collection of tactics puzzles in Slay the Spire. The goal of each puzzle is just "find the best line" rather than anything more specific -- what this means is for you to discover.

In general, these puzzles are very "weird", in that they are extremely unlikely (but possible) to come up in a real game.

Anything covered with a black rectangle is promised not to matter.

You can click on each image to toggle a "helper view", which shows the full text of each card and also displays tooltips describing some of the relics and status effects.
